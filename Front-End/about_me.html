About Us

Hand Companion is an innovative AI technology designed to enhance communication by utilizing advanced hand tracking capabilities. This AI system can accurately identify letters and gestures made by the user's hand movements. It then transcribes these gestures into words, allowing for efficient and intuitive communication.

What is Hand Companion

  • Natural-sounding speech
  • Aids those who are have auditory impairment
  • Serves as a translators to citizens
  • Can be accessed virtually anywhere
